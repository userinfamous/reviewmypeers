Welcome to Review My Peers!

An innovative platform for peer-to-peer review and feedback within the academic community.

About This Site

Review My Peers is a tool designed for students to engage in a constructive review process. By allowing students to provide and receive feedback, we aim to foster a collaborative environment for academic growth and excellence.

Getting Started

  1. See All Students: Explore student contributions in the 'See All Students' section to begin your peer reviews.
  2. See My Reviews: Track your submitted reviews under the 'See My Reviews' tab.
  3. See Instructor Reviews: Access feedback from instructors in the 'See Instructor Reviews' section to improve your reviewing skills.

To begin, select an option from the navigation menu and start your journey towards collaborative learning and improvement.

Why Choose Review My Peers?

  • Comprehensive Metrics: Unlike other platforms, we provide students with detailed metrics that assess various aspects of their peer reviews, including work ethic, timeliness, leadership skills, charisma, agreeableness, pragmatism, reliability, and trustworthiness.
  • Collaborative Learning: Our platform fosters a collaborative learning environment by enabling students to engage in constructive peer reviews, promoting academic growth and excellence.
  • Instructor Feedback: Access valuable feedback from instructors in the 'See Instructor Reviews' section, helping you improve your reviewing skills and enhance your academic performance.

Students Can Gauge

  • Work Ethic: Measures peer's opinion on your work ethic.
  • Timeliness: Measures how on time and how on top of things a student is.
  • Leadership Skills: Measures how good of a leader a student is to their peers.
  • Charisma: Measures how much charisma their peers think of them.
  • Agreeable: Measures how agreeable a person is.
  • Pragmatic: Measures if a person is likely to be pragmatic.
  • Reliability: Measures how reliable their peer thinks of them.
  • Trustworthiness: Measures how much their peer trusts them.

Instructors Can Be Gauged On

  • Teaching Ability: Measures the instructor's effectiveness in teaching and conveying concepts.
  • Hard Grader: Indicates how stringent or lenient the instructor is in grading.
  • Articulate: Assesses how clear and understandable the instructor's communication is.
  • Charisma: Reflects the instructor's ability to engage and inspire students.
  • Interesting: Gauges how interesting and engaging the instructor's lessons and materials are.
  • Timeliness: Measures the instructor's punctuality in starting classes, meeting deadlines, and responding to queries.
